School Profile

Markaz Academy, Mandia, run under the esteemed guidance of Markazul Ma'arif (NGO) Hojai and proudly acting as a unit of Ajmal CSR (Ajmal Foundation), is a Day Cum Residential English Medium School committed to providing quality education. Located in the heart of Mandia, our institution strives to create an environment where students can achieve excellence in learning, develop critical thinking, and build a strong moral foundation for their future endeavors.
